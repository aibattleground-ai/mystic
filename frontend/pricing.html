Frequently Asked Questions

How does paying in $ORC tokens work?
When you connect your Web3 wallet and pay with $ORC tokens, the smart contract automatically applies the discount corresponding to your tier. The tokens are then either burned or redistributed to node operators as part of our tokenomics structure.
What happens when I hit my API limit?
If you exceed your monthly request limit, future requests will respond with a 429 Too Many Requests status. You can upgrade your tier or purchase supplemental request bursts on your dashboard.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your fiat subscription at any time. For $ORC token payments, which operate via smart contract allowances, you can revoke the allowance at any point via your connected wallet.
