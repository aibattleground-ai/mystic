Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

At OracleEyes.AI, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal, astrological, and blockchain data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our AI destiny intelligence services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with you or your device. This includes:

  • Astrological Data: Names, dates of birth, times of birth, and places of birth provided for destiny readings.
  • Blockchain Data: Public Web3 wallet addresses, transaction hashes, and on-chain interactions related to $ORC token usage.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and interaction metrics on our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

The neural networks require historical and user-input data to generate accurate karmic probability scores. We use the collected data exclusively to:

  • Generate and deliver personalized Oracle Readings and Destiny Reports.
  • Process payments via blockchain smart contracts.
  • Train and fine-tune our proprietary AI models (all identifiable PII is stripped prior to training).
  • Comply with necessary legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Data Security and Decentralization

We employ enterprise-grade security measures. Sensitive astrological inputs are encrypted at rest using AES-256. Web3 wallet authorizations rely entirely on decentralized smart contracts; we do not store your private keys or seed phrases.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymized, aggregated demographic data with research partners. Standard transactional data is public by nature of the blockchain.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@oracleeyes.io.
